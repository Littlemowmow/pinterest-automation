AutoPin Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

What This App Does

AutoPin is a personal Pinterest automation tool that connects to your Google Drive and Pinterest accounts to help you schedule and post pins.

Data We Access

  • Google Drive: Read-only access to image files in a folder you specify.
  • Pinterest: Access to create pins and read your boards.

How Data Is Stored

OAuth tokens and image metadata are stored securely in a private database. Images are temporarily cached for posting. No data is shared with third parties beyond the APIs required to operate (Google, Pinterest, Anthropic).

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your accounts at any time from the Settings page, which removes all stored tokens. Contact the app owner to request full data deletion.

Contact

For questions, reach out to the app owner directly.
